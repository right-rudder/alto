## What Is a Discovery Flight?

A **Discovery Flight** is an introductory flight lesson that lets anyone, no experience required, sit in the pilot’s seat and take the controls under the guidance of a certified flight instructor. It’s hands-on, exciting, and a true taste of what pilot training is like at Alto Flight Academy.

During the flight, the recipient will:

- Explore the cockpit and aircraft controls  
- Learn the fundamentals of flight  
- Taxi, take the controls in the air, and enjoy views over Oklahoma City  
- Experience what real pilot training feels like  

Most importantly, they’ll walk away with a memory that lasts a lifetime.

A Discovery Flight isn’t just a ride, it’s an experience that sparks curiosity and opens the door to a completely new passion. It’s perfect for thrill seekers, learners, and anyone who dreams of trying something extraordinary.

Whether someone is 15 or 75, a Discovery Flight is accessible, safe, and thrilling. No prior aviation knowledge is needed, just a sense of adventure.

A Discovery Flight counts as the first official loggable hour toward a Private Pilot License. This makes it an ideal gift for anyone considering aviation professionally or as a lifelong hobby.

The stunning views of Oklahoma City from the cockpit, the hands-on flying experience, and that first feeling of lifting off the runway make for amazing photos and unforgettable moments.
